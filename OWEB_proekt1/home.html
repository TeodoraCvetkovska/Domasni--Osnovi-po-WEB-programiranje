Auckland

Discover the hidden gems of Auckland!

Auckland - a city in the North Island of New Zealand. Famous for its beatiful nature and stunning architecture. A place where calmness, fun and adventure are a garantee!

The City Center

The city center (also called the Viaduct) is recognized by its modern architecture. It is full with shops, bars, restaurants and night clubs.

Beaches

There are stunning beaches around Auckland. Some of them include: Long Bay-Okura, Milford Beach, Muriwai Beach, Tawharanui

Nature

Auckland is filled with parks and natural resesrve parks where you could go on a so called bush walk.
